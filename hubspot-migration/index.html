Risk Register

Key Risks

Priority-ordered risk register. P0 risks are go-live blockers — must be resolved before May 2.

P0 — Blocker

Compliance / Consent Data Gaps

Unsubscribe records must transfer 100% accurately. Failure = Israeli Privacy Protection Law violation + GDPR exposure.

P0 — Blocker

Aggressive 8-Week Timeline

Any delays cascade to go-live. Strict scope control essential — no scope additions without written change orders.

P1 — High

Marketo Decommissioned Early

Would leave a gap with no marketing platform. Exact decommissioning date must be confirmed immediately.

P1 — High

DNS / Domain Configuration Delays

Enterprise IT change management can slow DNS updates. Blocks email sending and LP hosting.

P1 — High

Scope Creep

Stakeholders may request beyond-scope features. Change control enforced at 500 NIS/hour for additions.

Medium

Email Deliverability Drop

New sending IP/domain requires warming strategy. Target: ≥95% deliverability within first 30 days.

Privacy & Legal

Compliance Requirements

Non-negotiable requirements. All must be satisfied before go-live.

Israeli Privacy Protection Law

Primary applicable law. All opt-out records must transfer with zero data loss.

GDPR

Applicable if marketing to EU contacts. Geographic distribution must be validated in Phase 1.

HubSpot DPA

Data Processing Agreement must be executed before any contact data is imported into HubSpot.

Unsubscribe Links

Every email requires a working unsubscribe link. HubSpot enforces this automatically.

Consent Checkboxes

All forms require explicit consent checkboxes. Legal must approve consent text.

Preference Center

Self-service subscription management must be live and tested before any email send.
